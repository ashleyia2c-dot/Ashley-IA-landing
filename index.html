Frequently asked

What exactly is Ashley?

A Windows desktop app with a character-driven AI companion inside. Ashley chats naturally, remembers you, reacts with moods and affection, can appear as a 2D/3D desktop pet, and can control your PC when you enable Actions.

What do I need to run her?

A Windows 10 or 11 PC (64-bit) and about 1.5 GB of disk space. For the AI itself: your own xAI or OpenRouter API key (pay-per-use, typically a few cents a day), or local Ollama, which is completely free. Ashley walks you through this on first launch.

How does the free trial work?

It's the full app — nothing locked, nothing removed — for 3 days or 300 messages, whichever comes first. No card, no account. When it ends, Ashley asks for a license key; your conversations and memories are kept, and unlocking brings them right back.

Which AI model does she use?

Your choice. Ashley supports xAI Grok, OpenRouter, and local Ollama. You bring your own key for cloud providers, or run Ollama locally for free. Capabilities such as vision, web search, speed, and language quality depend on the model you select.

Does she really wake up by voice?

Yes. Wake word detection runs locally on your PC using openWakeWord — no cloud microphone stream. Say "Ashley" or click the mic in the main app or pet mode. You can disable it any time.

Which operating systems are supported?

Windows 10 and 11 are fully supported. macOS and Linux builds are planned for after launch — if that's critical for you, drop us an email before buying.

Can I use her on multiple PCs?

Yes. Your license allows activation on up to 3 devices (desktop + laptop + 1 spare for reinstalls). Deactivate a slot from one PC to free it for another any time.

Windows warns about an "unknown publisher" — is that normal?

Yes. Ashley is a new indie app and the installer isn't code-signed yet, so Windows SmartScreen may warn on first run. Click "More info", then "Run anyway". Every installer is published openly on our GitHub releases page, and code signing is on the roadmap.

Refund policy?

Ashley comes with a free 3-day trial of the complete app, so you can be sure she clicks with you before paying anything. Because a license key can't be returned once it has been delivered, all sales are final. If something doesn't work on your machine, email us — we answer personally and we'll sort it out with you.

Is my data private?

Yes. Chat history, memories, diary and preferences live in local files on your PC — we don't have any servers and literally cannot see your data. If you use a cloud provider (xAI/OpenRouter), your messages go directly to them under their privacy policy. If you use Ollama, NOTHING leaves your PC — Ashley is fully offline.
